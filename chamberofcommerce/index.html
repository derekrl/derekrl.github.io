Purpose and Goals

As the voice for Provo business, we lead, convene, & influence to strengthen an already dynamic business climate that supports all sectors of our economy & delivers member value.

By 2065, Utah County will add 1 million people to its population, 85% from internal growth. We are planning for healthy & meaningful places for our kids to have places to work, live, learn, & play.

The Provo City Chamber of Commerce's mission is to build relationships, advocate principle-based public policy, & promote business & community prosperity.
