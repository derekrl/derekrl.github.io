Target Audience

This site is for those who live in (or visiting) the tri-city area of Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven, giving an overview of the latest weather forecasts and what to expect to see.

Personas

Victor Hansen. 33-year-old businessman, married with no children. Drives to work each weekday, once a month goes camping with wife and friend group at the lake. Uses weather forecast to pre-empt traffic, as well as planning for camping.

Julianna Jones. 52-year-old married mother of three. Works from home. Goes for jogs in the morning. Goes with family on road trips each spring and fall.

Scenarios

  • Is there supposed to be any rain tomorrow?
  • How strong will the wind be on the highway this morning?
  • Is it expected to go below freezing overnight?
  • Will the driveway need to be shoveled by tomorrow?
  • What time will the sun rise in the morning?
